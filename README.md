![HealthBridge Demo](assets/screenshot.png)

---

## 🚀 Overview

**HealthBridge** is a fintech-powered, AI-enhanced healthcare access platform designed to solve Nigeria's healthcare financing crisis. With fewer than 5% of Nigerians having health insurance, HealthBridge provides:

- **TNPL (Treat Now Pay Later)** — Healthcare credit with AI-powered approval
- **PharmVerify** — Drug authentication to fight counterfeit medicines
- **Surgery Fund** — Institutional & community crowdfunding for surgeries
- **HealthTokens** — Reward system for preventive health behaviors
- **Emergency Referral** — Find and connect to nearby hospitals

## 🎯 Why This Matters

**Nigeria's Healthcare Reality:**
- ❌ <5% have health insurance
- ❌ 70% of health spending is out-of-pocket
- ❌ 1 doctor per 2,500 patients (vs WHO 1:600)
- ❌ 15-25% of medicines are counterfeit

**HealthBridge solves this by:**
- ✅ Moving healthcare from "pay first" to "treat now"
- ✅ Fighting counterfeit drugs with PharmVerify
- ✅ Crowdfunding surgeries through institutional partnerships
- ✅ Incentivizing preventive care with HealthTokens
